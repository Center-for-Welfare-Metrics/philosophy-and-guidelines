# Contributing to WFI Methodological Standards

This repository is a living document. Its value depends on the active skepticism and contributions of our researchers. To maintain our "Quality Bar," please follow these workflows.

## 1. Reporting a "Strategic No" (Analytical Errors)
If you encounter a flaw in an AI output, a website explanation, or a methodological document:
1.  Navigate to the **Issues** tab.
2.  Click **New Issue** and select the **Strategic No (Rejection Log)** template.
3.  Fill out the **Recognition**, **Articulation**, and **Encoding** sections.
4.  Submit the issue. Your "No" will be reviewed and incorporated into our core constraints.

## 2. Proposing New Frameworks
If you are developing a new analytical standard (e.g., a new species-specific metric):
1.  Create a new branch or open a **Pull Request**.
2.  Add your draft to the `frameworks/` or `protocols/` directory as a Markdown (.md) file.
3.  Ensure the document follows our commitment to **Falsifiability** and **Stepwise Logic**.

## 3. Weekly Friction Reviews
Every Friday, an automated report of all "rejection-logs" is generated. Team members are expected to review the **Encoded Constraints** to ensure their shared "institutional taste" remains current.
